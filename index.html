SAVEUR
This is a compilation of ideas I have that will be implemented in my game "Saveur" which explores a small world where magic and color can be harnessed from flavors.
Savory
The flavor of pride, granting the consumer powers to summon creatures to their side, and grant themself and allies empowering buffs. Experience with this flavor also provides healing capabilities.
Summon
Buff
(RT) Perfom a ritual to summon creatures to fight alongside you on the battlefield.
(LT) Perform a chant to empower yourself and allies offensively and defensively.
Abilities
Active
Passive
  • Increase Summon Strength
  • Increase Healing On Hit
  • Increase Healing Over Time
  • Increase Max Summon Count
  • Increase Effect Duration
  • Increase Effect Strength
Spicy
The flavor of wrath, granting the consumer powers to cast fireballs and summon fireflies and pools from explosions. Experience with this flavor also provides acidic capabilites.
Spicy Glob
Spicy Bomb
(RT) Cast a small fireball at a target that bounces off terrain and causes acid buildup.
(LT + RT) Aim and lob a large fireball that will explode and summon fireflies around the area along with a lava pool.

Active
Passive

  • Increase Construct Duration
  • Increase Projectile Size
  • Increase Mana Regeneration
Salty
